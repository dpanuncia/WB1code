This is how you make Mac and Cheese

  1. Boil the pasta, drain. Make the cheese sauce by combining a fat (butter), and starch (flour), then whisking in the milk products.
  2. Cook the sauce until it's nice and thick. Add in shredded cheeses, stir well.
  3. Combine cheese sauce with cooked pasta.
